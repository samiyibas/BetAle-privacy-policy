Privacy Policy for Bet Ale
Last Updated: January 29, 2026

Semayawi Digitals ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Bet Ale mobile application. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information from users of our app.

1. Information We Collect
A. Personal Data
While using our app, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you, including:

Name
Email address
Phone number
Profile Picture
User IDs (for account management and identification)
B. Property Data
If you list a property, we collect details about that property, including its location, price, images, and specifications.

C. Device and Usage Data
We collect information about your device (e.g., model, OS version, unique device identifiers) and how you interact with our app for technical support and improvements.

2. Permissions Required
To provide full functionality, Bet Ale requires the following permissions:

Location (Precise): Used to show property listings near you and to allow you to set the exact location of properties you list on the map.
Camera/Gallery: Used for uploading property photos and your profile picture.
Notifications: Used to send you real-time updates about property statuses, messages, and app updates.
3. Data Encryption and Security
Encryption in Transit
All data collected by Bet Ale is encrypted in transit using standard Secure Socket Layer (SSL/TLS) technology. This ensures that your information remains private and secure while it travels between your device and our servers.

Security
We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

These third parties have access to your Personal Data only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.
